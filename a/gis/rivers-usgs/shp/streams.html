Description:
Abstract:
This is a 1:2,000,000 coverage of streams for the conterminous United States.

streams

Purpose:
This coverage was intended for use as a background display for the National Water Summary program.

Supplemental_Information:
Procedures_Used: The stream layer was extracted from the 1:2,000,000 Digital Line Graph files. Originally, each state was stored as a separate coverage. In this version, the individual state coverages all have been appended.

Revisions: Rev. 1.0 Original DLG conversion, state-by-state. Process_Date c1986 Rev. 2.0 Converted to the Data General workstation. All states joined into a single coverage. Process_Date 01/02/92 Reviews_Applied_to_Data: Rev. 1.0 has seen extensive use in a variety of studies.
