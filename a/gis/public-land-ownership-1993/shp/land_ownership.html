Description  
    Abstract
    The ownership layer for Oregon was compilied in digital format by
    The State Service Center for GIS in 1992 and 1993.  SSC/GIS received
    from the BLM the map seperates of the ownership layer from the Surface
    Management Status map series.  The SSC/GIS scanned and vectorized the
    map seperates into Arc/Info format.  The digital linework was then
    merged into the SSC/GIS digtial public land survey GIS layer.  The
    new set of polygons was cleaned up and attributed with a number in
    the database field "OWNER" to represent specific categories of ownership.
    Once the polygon topology was established and the polygon attribution
    was completed, the public land survey section lines were dissolved
    out of the ownership layer. Each 1:100000 scale map sheet was completed
    and QA/QC as an individual map sheet.  When all the sheets of Oregon
    were finished, they were merged into one statewide layer.  This involved
    edgematching each map sheet together to and dissolving their common borders
     
    Purpose
    This is data was compiled for use in a Geographic Information System

    Notes
    The map seperates for the ownership layer from the BLM Surface Management
    Status maps were scanned and vectorized in Arc/Info format GIS data.
    This digital linework was merged with a digital Public Land Survey GIS lay
    and then each polygon was attributed with the proper ownership code.
    The PLS section lines were dissolved away based on the ownership code.  Each
    quadsheet was then QA/QC for correct polygon attributions.  The final
    step involved edgematching the individual quadsheets into one statewide
    layer and dissolving away the quadsheet boundaries.

 Overview_Description 
    Entity_and_Attribute_Overview 
    The database field "OWNER" is a numeric field that holds the information
    about the particular agency responsible for the land.  The ownership codes
     
    1 = Bureau of Land Management
    2 = Oregon and California Lands administered by the BLM
    3 = US Forest Service
    4 = Oregon And California Lands administered by the USFS
    5 = State of Oregon
    6 = Unused Code
    7 = Military or Army Corp. of Engineers
    8 = Bankhead and Jones Land Use Lands
    9 = Indian Reservations
    10 = County Lands
    11 = National Grasslands
    12 = National Parks and Monuments
    13 = National Wildlife Refuges
    9999 = Private/Non-Governmental Lands
